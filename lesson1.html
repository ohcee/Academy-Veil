Lesson 1: What is Veil?

Veil is a privacy-focused cryptocurrency that launched in 2019. It uses a combination of RingCT and stealth addresses to keep amounts and participants private, along with Dandelion++ to obfuscate your IP address during transaction broadcasting.

Veil has a maximum supply of 300 million coins and is powered by a hybrid 50/50 Proof-of-Stake and Proof-of-Work system. The Proof-of-Work mining is further split across three algorithms:

  • ProgPoW: 35 percent of daily blocks
  • RandomX: 10 percent of daily blocks
  • SHA256d: 5 percent of daily blocks
  • Staking: 50 percent of daily blocks

This combination supports decentralization across different hardware types while rewarding both miners and stakers.
