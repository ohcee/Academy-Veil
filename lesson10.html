Lesson 10: How to Stake Zerocoin Mints

Veil uses a staking system based on Zerocoin mints. Only minted coins can stake and earn rewards. Once you've minted into one of the four supported denominations (10, 100, 1000, 10000), you're ready to begin staking.

  • Open the Veil Core Wallet
  • Enable staking
  • Keep the wallet running and online
  • Use high-value mints like 10000 for better staking performance
  • Let rewards accumulate and compound to increase your staking weight

To check if staking is active, look for the **slider icon** in the wallet interface.

If staking is not active, verify that your wallet is synced, unlocked, and that your coins are mature and properly minted.
