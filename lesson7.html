Lesson 7: Converting Coins & Minting Zerocoin for Staking

Veil supports multiple types of coins. To stake and earn rewards, your coins must be minted into Zerocoin. It is strongly recommended to use Veils RingCT privacy system when minting. Zerocoin minting uses four fixed denominations: 10, 100, 1000, or 10000.

You can mint Zerocoin from any coin type: Basecoin, Stealth, or RingCT. However, for maximum privacy, you should always mint from RingCT.

- Minting from Basecoin or Stealth will create valid Zerocoins, but outside observers might be able to trace the original input.
- Minting from RingCT ensures that the amount, sender, and recipient remain private.

To stake:

  1. Use the Veil Core Wallet
  2. Convert your coins to RingCT
  3. Mint them into one of the four Zerocoin denominations
  4. Leave the wallet open and unlocked for staking
