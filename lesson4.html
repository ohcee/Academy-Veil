Lesson 4: How to Stake Veil

Staking is the process of earning rewards by holding and minting your Veil coins in the Core Wallet. It helps secure the network and lets you earn passive income. Your coins remain in your wallet and are never sent to the network.

To stake:

  1. Download and install the Veil Core Wallet
  2. Transfer coins into the wallet
  3. Convert coins to RingCT and mint them
  4. Unlock the wallet for staking and keep it open

The more coins you stake, the better your chances of earning consistent rewards. Staking also supports Veils decentralized and energy-efficient consensus model.

1. What do you need to do to stake Veil?

Use the light wallet
Use a mining rig
Use the core wallet

2. What is the benefit of staking?

Lower transaction fees
Earn passive Veil rewards
Hide your address from everyone

3. What happens to your coins during staking?

They are sent to an exchange
They are spent immediately
They remain in your wallet and help secure the network
