Lesson 4: How to Stake Veil

Staking is the process of earning rewards by holding and minting your Veil coins in the Core Wallet. It helps secure the network and lets you earn passive income. Your coins remain in your wallet and are never sent to a third party.

How to Stake Veil

  1. Download and install the Veil Core Wallet.
  2. Transfer coins into your wallet.
  3. Convert coins to RingCT and mint them.
  4. Unlock your wallet for staking and leave it running.
