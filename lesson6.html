Lesson 6: Basecoin vs Stealth vs RingCT

Veil supports multiple privacy layers: Basecoin, Stealth, and RingCT.

  • Basecoin: Transparent like Bitcoin. Sender, receiver, and amount are visible.
  • Stealth: Hides the recipients address while still showing the amount.
  • RingCT: Hides sender, receiver, AND amount, Veils strongest privacy option.

You can freely convert between these types in the Veil Core Wallet. RingCT provides full privacy, while Stealth and Basecoin remain useful for flexibility, exchanges, and interoperability.
