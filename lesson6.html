Lesson 6: Basecoin vs Stealth vs RingCT

Veil supports multiple privacy layers: Basecoin, Stealth, and RingCT. Each offers a different level of privacy and utility:

  • Basecoin: Fully transparent like Bitcoin. Sender, receiver, and amount are public.
  • Stealth: Hides the recipients address but still shows the amount.
  • RingCT: Hides the sender, recipient, and amount. This offers maximum on-chain privacy.

You can convert between these privacy types within the Veil Core Wallet depending on how you want to use your coins. RingCT offers the most privacy, but Stealth and Basecoin serve important roles for compatibility and flexibility.
