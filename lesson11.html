Lesson 11: How to Contribute to the Veil Project

Veil is an open-source, community-driven project focused on privacy, decentralization, and financial freedom. You do not need to be a developer to make an impact. There are many ways to contribute:

  • Use Veil: Stake, mine, transact, and report your experiences.
  • Report bugs or suggest improvements: Use Discord or GitHub.
  • Translate and educate: Help make tutorials or localize content for your region.
  • Promote Veil: Share guides, wallet tips, or community events on social media.
  • Build with Veil: Code new tools, submit pull requests, or test experimental features.
  • Join the community: Chat and collaborate on Discord, our main hub for real-time support and teamwork.
