Lesson 8: Denominations and Staking Efficiency

Veils Zerocoin staking system uses fixed denominations when you mint coins: 10, 100, 1000, and 10000. When converting RingCT to Zerocoin, you must choose one of these predetermined sizes.

While all denominations can stake, larger ones perform much better because:

  • Higher-value mints have a stronger chance of being selected to stake
  • Large denominations reduce fragmentation
  • Your wallet runs faster and more efficiently

You can check live staking distribution here: Veil Stats.

1. Which Zerocoin denomination is most efficient for staking?

10
100
10000

2. Why is staking with many small denominations not ideal?

It gives higher rewards
It uses more memory and is less efficient
It makes RingCT fail

3. What should a user do before minting for best staking results?

Convert balance into as many 10s as possible
Stake from Basecoin
Consolidate balance and mint a 10000 if possible
